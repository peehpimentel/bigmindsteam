About us

We are an esports organization and lifestyle company founded in 2015 by amateurs players, developers and experts in Digital Ad Trafficker. Our family is constituted by every corner of Brazil, and because of that we believe that people are the new brands and it is through stories that we will reach the public's heart. We build our content on pillars like esports, games and digital entertainment for the new and old generation, creating cross-platform daily content and believing in providing the means for players and talent to become digital celebrities. Despite our diversity as entertainment, our biggest focus is on conquering the competitive Counter-Strike: Global Offensive scene.
